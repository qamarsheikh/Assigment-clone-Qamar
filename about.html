About Our Clinic

Horyaal Hospital is multi-specialty hospital with 150 beds, located within heart of Mogadishu along the Tarbuunka Road, Horyaal Hospital will remain one of the most easily and securely accessible private hospitals within the city. It’s location within the Tarbuunka Road area allows for a quick and safe access by both the corporate class and general public without undermining both security and the quality of health care provided. Horyaal hospital was established in 2024 an era when Somalia so badly needed a health care upgrade, as it picks up pieces of its structures that were shattered during the conflict; the hospital’s mission is to provide high quality yet very affordable health care to the people living in Somalia. The hospital is governed by the same Board of trustees and by Somali intellectuals made up of local leaders committed to the importance of philanthropy and fulfilling the hospital’s mission.
